Our story

Made from scratch,
made with care

What started as a weekend hobby in a small kitchen has grown into something truly special. Aurabloom was born from a love of slow evenings, warm light, and the way a single candle can change the mood of an entire room.

Every candle is hand-poured in small batches, using natural soy wax, cotton wicks, and fragrance blends we've spent months perfecting.
